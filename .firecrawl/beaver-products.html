Our menu isn’t just about pastries! From savoury bites to satisfying meals, we’ve got options to fuel your adventure. Whether you're looking for a quick snack, a sweet fix, or a comforting meal, there’s something delicious waiting for you.

Ice Cream

While we offer vegan-friendly options, our kitchen is not certified vegan, and cross-contamination may occur. Our products or ingredients could contain or come into contact with peanuts, nuts, milk, butter, eggs, wheat, soy, tropical oil, meat products, or other allergens. Availability may vary by location, and ingredients are subject to change. Please look for allergen icons above each item, and click on any product to view full allergen details. If you have food allergies or dietary restrictions, we strongly recommend speaking with a staff member before placing your order.
